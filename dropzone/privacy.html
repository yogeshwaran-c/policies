DROPZONE

Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-05-20

What we collect

Nothing.

We do not collect, store, or transmit personally identifiable information. There are no accounts, no servers we operate, no third-party analytics, and no advertising or tracking SDKs.

What stays on your device

For the App to remember your preferences and statistics between sessions, the following information is saved locally on your device only:

  • Settings — theme, sound and haptic preferences, control configuration (DAS, ARR, soft-drop speed), display options.
  • Statistics — high scores, total games played, lines cleared, combos, and recent game results per mode.
  • Onboarding state — whether you have completed the first-run tutorial.

This data is stored using device-local key-value storage. It never leaves your device, never reaches our servers (we do not operate any), and is deleted when you uninstall the App. You can clear stats from Settings → Stats → Reset all stats at any time.

Permissions

Permission Why we need it
INTERNET Required by the Android runtime to load the React Native bundle on first launch. We do not make any network calls of our own.
VIBRATE Used to deliver haptic feedback during gameplay. Disable in Settings → Audio & Haptics.

DROPZONE does not request microphone, storage, location, contacts, camera, or other sensitive permissions.

Children's privacy

DROPZONE is suitable for all audiences. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13, and the App is rated Everyone / PEGI 3.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change what data is processed (for example by adding optional analytics, cloud-saved leaderboards, or in-app purchases), we will update this policy and clearly disclose the change inside the App before the new behavior takes effect.
