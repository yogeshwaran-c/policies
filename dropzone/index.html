DROPZONE

Retro · Modern · Offline

A polished single-player tetromino puzzler for Android. Four modes, modern guideline scoring with T-spins and perfect clears, neon-on-dark retro aesthetic. No ads, no tracking, no accounts — just the game.

FOUR MODES

Pick Your Tempo

Each mode has its own pace and its own personal best to chase.

∞
MARATHON
Clear 200 lines with progressive speed. The long-form mode — settle in.
⚡
SPRINT
Clear 40 lines as fast as you can. Race your best time, lockdown your PB.
◷
ULTRA
Two minutes. One mission: maximum score. Risk it all on every drop.
◐
ZEN
Endless. No game over. No timer. Just play, until you want to stop.
WHY DROPZONE

Built for Players

The features people care about, and the ones they shouldn't have to worry about.

CRAFT
Guideline scoring
Hand-tuned SRS with T-spin detection, back-to-back bonuses, combos and perfect clears.
CRAFT
60 FPS Skia rendering
Native Skia + Reanimated power every frame. Animations stay smooth on mid-range Android devices.
CRAFT
Tunable controls
DAS, ARR and soft-drop factor are all adjustable. Competitive players, this is for you.
PRIVACY
No tracking, ever
Zero analytics, zero ads, zero third-party SDKs. Stats stay on your device.
PRIVACY
Plays fully offline
After install you never need a network connection. No accounts, no sign-in, no cloud.
ACCESS
WCAG 2.2 AA
Reduced motion, dynamic type, screen-reader announcements, lefty-mirror controls, photosensitive-friendly modes.
CRAFT
Audio + haptic pair
Every input — move, rotate, lock, line clear — gets a paired audio cue and a haptic tap. Listen and feel the game.
ACCESS
Dark and light themes
Both themes are calibrated for WCAG AA contrast. Follows your system setting automatically, or pin one from Settings.
CRAFT
One-handed portrait
Locked portrait orientation, controls sized for thumbs, hold and rotate within easy reach. Designed for the commute.
